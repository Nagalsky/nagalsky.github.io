Using (Capitalization)

Rule

This word must begin with a capital letter.

Explanation

A capital letter is required here.

Definition

Certain words must be written with a capital letter, such as the pronoun and proper names (such as personal names, last names, and geographical names). The first word of a sentence must also begin with a capital letter.

Example

  1. Did i [I] tell you the latest news already?
  2. my [My] wife and I would like to invite you for dinner
  3. Did you meet with paul [Paul] and denise [Denise] yesterday?
