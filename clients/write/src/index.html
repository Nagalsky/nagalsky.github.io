WHO ARE WE?

For almost 29 years now, we have strived to deliver quality concrete and asphalt services safely, on time, and within budget across the Southeast. If you need a contractor for any type of concrete or asphalt work, we can handle it for you -- from installing new parking lots or repairing or sealcoating existing ones, to pouring concrete slabs and foundations, to helping your facility meet ADA compliance requirements, and more.

From new construction to maintenance/repair, if it's concrete or asphalt, we can do it. We have offices in Memphis, Nashville, Birmingham, Chattanooga, and Huntsville and serve the entire Southeast.

For a contractor that provides highly skilled work on time and at competitive prices, call Wright Construction to discuss your next project today.
