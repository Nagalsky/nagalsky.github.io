The KMZ Token

Karmaza enables users world­wide to participate in social fundraising campaigns with a decentralize platform that accepts all major crypto currencies.

Donors can support campaigns with all major cryptocurrencies and even US Dollars. All currencies will be automatically converted into KMZ tokens.

Beneficiaries will fundraise KMZ tokens for their campaigns in a locked­wallet.

Once the fundraising goal has been met, users can trade their KMZ tokens for US Dollars or other currencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin.

How Karmaza Works

World Wide Acceptance

With the power of cryptocurrencies, Karmaza is able to accept beneficiaries/campaigners and donors from all countries, with absolutely no limitations. This will enable every person, worldwide, to fully access and utilize the power of social fundraising.

Full Transparency

All beneficiary / campaigner / donor interactions and transactions are managed by a smart contract and are transparent to the community by its very nature.

Zero Fees

Karmaza does not take fees from any beneficiary / campaigner / donor for using the platform.

Fraud Protection

The community is the guard. Trusted community members verify the campaigns and smart contracts do the rest.
