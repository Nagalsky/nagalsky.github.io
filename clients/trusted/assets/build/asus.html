KEY FEATURES

A slow internet connection can become a real bummer. Primarily if you work from home, need a fast internet connection for daily use, or you’re just an avid gamer. Have you ever thought about WiFi extenders? Meet ASUS RP-AC55 - a dual-band wireless WiFi extender that will speed up your internet connection up to 1200Mbps and cover up to 3000 sq. ft. area. RP-AC55 is powered by the newest 802.11ac chipset, enabling ultra-fast results. Plus, this chipset delivers three times faster internet speed than the standard 802.11n (Wireless-N)! In addition, this WiFi repeater works with any router in the world, helping boost the range and improve the coverage while at the same time eliminating all dead zones around your house.

DESIGN & PORTABILITY

ASUS RP-AC55 maintains two external antennas for specifically enhancing WiFi signal in your whole home area. What’s more, this extender has LED signal indicators which can be helpful when finding the best spot to place it for the ultimate performance. Furthermore, AC55 is relatively small, so it definitely won’t bother you when deciding where to put it as it will perfectly blend in. Also, this internet extender supports a variety of devices with operational systems like Windows and Mac OS.

EASE OF USE

When setting it up, you can use a one-click setup by utilizing WPS buttons which is a rapid and automatic setup. But suppose you wish to access advanced settings. In that case, you can easily connect this RP-AC55 extender to your PC via an Ethernet cable and, in this way, get access to the web-based configuration menu. But there’s more. For a more efficient setup, you can also use the ASUS Extender app, which takes only a couple of seconds to transfer your router’s login information to this extender. In addition, it gives easy instructions to help you find an ideal place for your RP-AC55.

VALUE

This internet extender does the job, for sure! ASUS RP-AC55 provides a stable connection and significantly faster streaming, gaming, and general browsing. Due to its advanced technology and LED indicators, you’ll be able to find the best spot for RP-AC55 to achieve the most optimal internet performance. Then, simply click the WPS buttons for automatic setup and enjoy an ultra-fast internet connection!
