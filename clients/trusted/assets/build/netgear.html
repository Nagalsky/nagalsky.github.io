KEY FEATURES

Are you tired of ‘dead’ zones and not being able to stream and browse the internet without experiencing buffering? Do not worry - we got you covered. Meet Netgear EX6120 - a dual-band WiFi range extender that will boost your internet connection and eliminate all ‘dead zones’ around your house. This Netgear WiFi booster extends connection up to 1,500 sq. ft., in this way, reaching even dead spots around the house. What’s more, Netgear connects up to 25 different devices to the network minimizing external inferences to maximize speed to these connected devices. The Netgear EX6120 has also got an Ethernet port that connects a wired device for faster connection and streaming. Regarding the security aspect, Netgear EX6120 is supported by WEP and WPA/WPA2 wireless security protocols.

DESIGN & PORTABILITY

Netgear EX6120 has a discreet design enabling wall plug method allowing the device to stay out of sight. Moreover, it comes with two external antennas, which deliver more outstanding performance and speed. Also, Netgear carries a universal compatibility feature, meaning that it works efficiently with any of your chosen WiFi routers. Finally, Netgear was designed with two available WiFi modes, which enable either the extension of the existing WiFi connection or making a brand-new internet access point.

EASE OF USE

Netgear EX6120 takes only a couple of minutes to set it up. Simply press WPS button on this extender, then press the same button on your WiFi router. This WPS button on the extender will show a solid LED light indicating a good connection between the router and Netgear extender. Make sure your new internet extender and your router are placed in the same room.

VALUE

All in all, Netgear EX6120 is a hassle-free internet connection extending device which will become handy when eliminating ‘dead zones’ around the house and boost up the speed. Do not worry about your router! Netgear will connect to any existing WiFi router; simply connect by using WPS buttons on both devices. However, if you want an even faster connection, connect your chosen device via Ethernet port for an uninterrupted internet connection.
