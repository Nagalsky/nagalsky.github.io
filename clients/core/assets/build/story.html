Our Story

Our Mission:

To build exceptional homes, outstanding communities, and trusted relationships.

For years Junior McGaha has been building homes as an avocation while he pursued his career in IT engineering. His gorgeous custom homes were built with an engineer’s eye for going the extra mile and extraordinary attention to detail. In 2018 Junior and his wife, Vickie took the leap of faith and followed their passion for creating amazing homes and launched Core Homes. Making their home buyers building experience nothing short of exceptional created growing success simply by word of mouth. In 2020 the McGaha’s grew their team of homebuilding professionals to expand their capabilities and Core Home’s success continues to grow to this day.

We believe in home and life well lived.
