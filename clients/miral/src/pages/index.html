For the first time this year, and as part of its employee engagement program, our Human Resources team introduced ‘Miral Kids Day’ – an opportunity for colleagues to bring along their little ones to the office almost every Thursday throughout July and August. The event was successful among employees and kids alike, as we welcomed a total of 54 child throughout the two months.

QUICK TIPS ON SPOTTING FAKE NEWS:
Fake content has become a daily reality of life online, with hundreds of sites creating false or distorted stories for political or personal gain. Here are some quick tips to help you spot fake news:
  1. Beware of fake website addresses as some sites may try to impersonate real news outlets. For instance, one fake news site impersonated ABC news, but with a URL which read ABC.com.co, rather than abcnews.go.com.
  2. Look out for suspicious dates as false news stories often include wrong timelines.
  3. Look for unusual spellings and mistakes.
  4. Be wary of headlines which are trying to provoke anger or cause a reaction.
  5. If you are unsure, double check with a source you trust.
