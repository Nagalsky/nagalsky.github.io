Shipping Policy

We offer free worldwide shipping unless specified otherwise in the sales page. We dispatch the goods from our warehouse in Vilnius, Lithuania.

Delivery will take up to 30 days and delivery delays can occasionally occur. We are not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are for your account (tariffs, taxes, etc.). If you received your order damaged, please contact us via support@techfibrr.com to resolve the issue. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before reaching out.

If you have not received your item after 30 days since your order was processed or have any additional questions regarding shipping, please contact us at support@techfibrr.com
