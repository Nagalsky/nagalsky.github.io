the tax filing on the go

taptax™ is a mobile app that allows you to send your tax information to your professional tax preparer from anywhere – no need for an office visit! You can save time and get your refund quickly.

Secure

Securely transmit your tax documents and information to your taptax preparer using a provided unique Tax ID number for confidential communication.

Easy To Use

Simply snap a picture of your tax documents, send them to your taptax preparer and sign your tax return all from your mobile device.

Convenient

Quickly and securely send your tax documents to your taptax preparer from your mobile device without ever having to visit an office.

how does it work

Simply snap photos of your tax documents. The app will provide your unique Tax Number, which you will give to your taptax preparer who will then obtain the documents and information you’ve uploaded into the app.

Sit back and enjoy knowing your taxes are being prepared by a certified tax specialist. You will be notified once your taxes are done.
