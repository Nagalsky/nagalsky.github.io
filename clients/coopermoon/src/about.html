CopperMoon® About Us

CopperMoon®, headquartered in West Point, Georgia, is an original manufacturer of premium landscape lighting products.

The business is owned, operated and managed by Doug Shumate & family. CopperMoon® is committed to providing its customers a level of customer service that is unparalleled in the landscape lighting business. By coupling this customer service with a time tested product portfolio that has demonstrated quality, customer acceptance and reliability, we intend to be the leading provider of premium landscape lighting products. In a time where getting someone to simply make a decision can be a customer’s biggest challenge, CopperMoon® can commit to you that will not be a challenge that is encountered by our customers. We welcome communication from anyone so please contact Doug Shumate with any questions or concerns that you may encounter as you consider CopperMoon® for your premium landscape lighting needs.
