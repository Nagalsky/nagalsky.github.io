How it works

1

Keep coins you need for daily transactions in your crypto wallet.

2

Place excess coins on deposit with the Central Bank.Deposits are offered in five maturities: 12 hours, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days and 30 days. These periods of time signify how long you will have to wait before you get your deposit back after asking for it.

3

Earn riskless interest.Each of the deposits pays a different interest rate, determined in every block. The more popular a particular deposit is, the lower its interest rate.

4

Ask for your deposit back when you need it.The Central Bank will send it to your address with a delay that equals the maturity of your chosen deposit.
