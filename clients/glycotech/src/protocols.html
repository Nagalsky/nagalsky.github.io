Measurement of Cell Adhesion Under Static Conditions

John L. Magnani~GlycoTech Corporation, Rockville, Maryland 20850

Many different molecules have been described to promote cell adhesion including several cell surface carbohydrate-binding proteins. Measuring call adhesion in the convenient 96-well microtiter plate format is difficult due to the shear forces generated by washing we wells. This protocol introduces the use of a liquid-filled was chamber that separates unbound cells by gravity thereby eliminating unclontrolled shear forces and passage of adherent cells through a liquid/air interface.
