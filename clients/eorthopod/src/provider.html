Dr. Richard Bowman, M.D.

A West Virginia native, Dr. Bowman received a bachelor’s degree in biology with a minor in chemistry and English from Marshall University in Huntington, West Virginia, before enrolling in the West Virginia University School of Medicine. After earning his medical degree, he completed a residency in physical medicine and rehabilitation. Dr. Bowman speaks across the country about the use of spinal cord stimulation, radiofrequency ablation, and many other ground breaking procedures that help patients with chronic pain. He is board certified by the American Board of Pain Medicine and the World Institute of pain (WIP).

At Roane General Hospital, we know that suffering from chronic pain can be draining and debilitating. The good news is that safe solutions for managing pain – solutions that eliminate or minimize the need for medications – now exist close to home thanks to Dr. Richard Bowman.
