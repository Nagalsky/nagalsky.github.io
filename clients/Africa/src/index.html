About Us

Foreign Desk|Africa is a supply chain manager focused on purchasing physical commodities and transforming these into customised products for our customers

Our business consists of logistics and transportation, warehousing, price risk management and hedging, commodity processing and blending.

Our business is oriented towards partnering with, rather than competing against producers. We provide best-in-class execution as we aim to be the best company in the world moving physical commodities from producers to consumers

We market, process, finance and transport key commodities, connecting low-cost producing regions with high-demand growth markets.

Our Vision

  • An empowered Africa with a sustainable income
  • A competitive Africa in the global commodity market
  • Africa a hub for processed / finished products
  • An empowered Africa with a sustainable economic growth and social stability

Our Mission

  • Improve access to market for smallholder farmers
  • Increase smallholder farmers yields
  • Reduce post-harvest loss for smallholder farmers
  • Improve smallholder farmers livelihoods and food and income security

Sustainability

Our Values drives everything we do and, as a result, our close attention to safety,
quality, relationships, traceability and training.
Safety Product Quality Relationships with growers Traceability Training

Health and safety is a core priority for the company. Its SafetyFirstAgri initiative aims to eliminate contamination.

Food safety and quality management procedures help ensure products meet international standards.

By managing the supply chain and maintaining close relationships with growers - the company aims to ensure that all products are consistent, reliable and high quality.

Strong and positive relationships with growers are vital. Foreign Desk|Africa primarily buys from producers and farming cooperatives where possible; this allows it to engage with growers – and manage quality from the farm to the customer.

We act responsibly, and in compliance with rules, when sourcing commodities from third-party suppliers. We ensure our commodity are traceable to the source of origin

Smallholders live in some of the most remote parts of the world where often roads are nothing more than dirt tracks. Collecting one sack of cocoa or coffee from every farmer is highly inefficient. We therefore work with farmers to help them form farmer groups or co-operatives where they bring their crop to a central warehouse. Here the crop is weighed and stamped, ready for collection by Foreign Desk|Africa. This is supported by numerous advances in technology.

As well as helping smallholder farmers to improve their yields we provide training on quality and reducing post-harvest losses. This is critical because good quality means they can command a premium, whilst preventing wastage means they improve their incomes. In addition to training farmers in Good Agricultural Practices, we invest in farmer resource centres, warehouses, drying floors to prevent soil contamination and solar driers.
