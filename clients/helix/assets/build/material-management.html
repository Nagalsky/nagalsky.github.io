Manage warehouses and depots

Pick, assemble and ship materials faster and more reliably using our complete warehouse management solution. Monitor stock levels across multiple depots at the component level.

Make management of orders, deliveries, returns and issues of stock a breeze and get peace of mind in the knowledge that everything is recorded and logged.

Handle allocation

Order, requisition and issue parts with confidence knowing the information you have is always up-to-date. By importing your project’s latest design data you can ensure that the components allocated are the components that go to site.

Make sure that your team are on the same page by viewing cross sections and allocation as well as component and assembly drawings on site using our mobile app.

Make requisitions simple

Supercharge your process by allowing engineers to requisition materials at the touch of a button.

By linking requisitions to the latest design data you can ensure that the materials requested match the latest design revision.

Connect your teams and work collaboratively

Connect engineers, warehouse managers, material coordinators and more to work together, share data and get work done.

View design drawings on site

Once the latest design revision is imported cross sections, allocation, component drawings and assembly drawings can all be viewed on site ensuring that every member of your team has access to the latest information.

Receive as-built data directly from site

Stay up-to-date by receiving key as-built information straight from site. Custom reports allow you to quickly review key data such as RECOS, foundation, steel or cantilever as-builts and modify allocation accordingly.

See the big picture with custom-built reports

Get insights in an instant by using custom built reports. What to know the total value of your stock? Or maybe you want to forecast the total cost of an allocation… we’ve got you covered.
