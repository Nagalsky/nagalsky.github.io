About Us

We're a a membership service for investors that aims to make your life easier.

I'm Nicolai. I built this service in 2020, because I was frustrated with the daily process of keeping up with my portfolio.

Jumping from site to site, scrolling through news and Twitter feeds. With a handful of stocks it quickly added up a lot of hours a week.

I've spent the past 20 years developing trading applications, so I knew the domain and I realized I could help a lot of people simplify their lives a little. Every day.

If you have any questions about the service, or anything else, please feel free to email me at nick@stockhawk.app and I'll get back to you.
