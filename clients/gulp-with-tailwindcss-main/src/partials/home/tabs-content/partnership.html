Partnerships & Ecosystems

As we look to develop these new critical capabilities for Shell, one of the foundational principles of powering progress has been working customer backwards.

  • Building deep links with credible partners in academia, the start-up ecosystem or in major tech firms
  • Be a key player in critical national initiatives across countries
  • Ensure that what we build is commercially relevant and explore licensing options
  • Developing methods for us to easily open source our projects, and make open-source contributions
  • Leverage Shell.ai as a digital innovation brand; internally & externally
