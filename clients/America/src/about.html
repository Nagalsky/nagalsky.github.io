ABOUT US
DOLOR SIT AMET

America's Hearth and Patio Warehouse, (US Stove Factory Outlet), located in Chattanooga, TN, is family owned and operated and has served the Tri-state area since 1983. We have been "Keeping America Warm" since 1869.

We are the only authorized outlet for United States Stove Company. We also have the area's largest heating showroom with a very broad and attractive selection of stoves, fireplaces, furnaces, gas logs, heaters, patio accessories and grills. We have GAS, WOOD, PELLET and even COAL options.
We are one of the few in the area that advertise our factory direct pricing. You can't beat the sales.. and we back it up with our reliable, friendly service.
Let us help you explore a variety of options to improve your home's value, reduce your heating bill or simply add a touch of decorative style.
