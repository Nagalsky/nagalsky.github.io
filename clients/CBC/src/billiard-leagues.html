BILLIARD LEAGUES

Join one of our pool leagues! Opened in 1982, the Chattanooga Billiard Club has become the home to fun and exciting co-ed billiard league play every week for both APA 8-Ball formats and BCA 9-Ball league formats. Leagues are a great way to improve your game and meet new people. All of our leagues are team leagues so it gives you a great opportunity to build camaraderie. Most of the leagues we run are handicapped for beginner and intermediate players to compete with other players regardless of skill levels.
